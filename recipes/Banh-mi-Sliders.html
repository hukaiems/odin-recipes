Banh Mi Slider

Description

Vietnamese-inspired prok and beef banh mi sliders.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats

Steps

  1. Gently mix beef, pork, oats, bread
  2. Heat a frying pan over medium high heat.
  3. While patties fry, toast slider buns in a toaster yntil lightly browned.

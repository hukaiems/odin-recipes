Italian Sausage

Description

This is the simple and delicious one-pan sausage and peppers recipe has been used in our family for years and years now. Serve in a roll, or use as a topping for pizza or pasta.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook sausage in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Melt butter in the saame skillet. Stir in onions
  3. Return sausage slices to the skillet.
